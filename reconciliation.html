Critical Process Issues

1. No Bank Integration HIGH IMPACT
Impact: Requires 15-20 hours/week of manual Excel matching between ERA data and bank deposits. High error rate and missed payments.
Revenue Risk: Estimated $50,000-$100,000/year in missed or delayed payment identification
2. Paper Check Dependency HIGH IMPACT
Impact: 100-150 paper checks per month require complete manual entry. No OCR, no automation, no integration with lockbox services.
Staff Time: 12-20 hours/month on data entry alone
3. Delayed Payment Visibility HIGH IMPACT
Impact: 15-30 day lag between bank deposit and system posting prevents accurate cash flow forecasting and working capital management.
Business Risk: Cannot accurately report daily or weekly revenue performance
4. Patient Payment Processing MEDIUM IMPACT
Impact: No integration with online payment processors. Patient portal payments require manual reconciliation.
Time Cost: 10-12 hours/week on patient payment posting
5. Month-End Reconciliation Burden HIGH IMPACT
Impact: Monthly close requires 3-5 days of intensive reconciliation work. Typical variance of $5,000-$15,000 must be manually investigated.
Efficiency Loss: Staff cannot focus on other RCM activities during month-end
Automation Opportunity: With proper bank API integration and RPA deployment, 70-80% of current manual reconciliation work could be eliminated, freeing up 30-40 hours per month for higher-value activities like denial management and AR follow-up.

Recommended Process Improvements

Bank API Integration
Target: Automated

Solution: Implement direct bank API integration (Plaid, Finicity, or bank-native API) to automatically import daily deposits into AdvancedMD or reconciliation middleware.

Time Savings: 15-20 hours/week

ROI Timeline: 3-6 months

RPA for ERA-Deposit Matching
Target: Automated

Solution: Deploy RPA bot to automatically match ERA amounts to bank deposits using intelligent matching algorithms (exact match, fuzzy match, batch totals).

Time Savings: 10-15 hours/week

Accuracy Improvement: 95%+ match rate vs 70-80% manual

Lockbox Service with OCR
Target: Automated

Solution: Implement lockbox service with EOB scanning and OCR to automatically process paper checks and remittances.

Time Savings: 12-20 hours/month

Cost: $0.50-$1.00 per item vs manual labor cost

Implementation Priority: Bank API integration should be the #1 priority for 2025. This single improvement addresses the largest manual burden and provides foundation for further automation opportunities.
